Toddlers:
Ages: 1-2

Children begin to explore and discover the world around them. They develop independence during meal times and build large motor skills. We also focus on improving their language skills.

Twos:
Ages: 2-3

Children are more independent and also begin to bond with friends. Activities are designed to meet their high-energy levels. There are a lot of opportunities to build on their language (over 50 words), and social skills.

Preschool:
Ages: 3-4

Children start to work and play as a team. They learn through hands-on experiences and are introduced to literacy and number concepts.There is a radip development in language skills with over 250 words.

Pre K:
Ages: 4-5

Children build effective communication skills and are able to speak complex sentences.They engage in more pretend play practicing their social skills. They also develop academic skills needed for elementary schools.

We offer flexible schedules of 2 days, 3 days or 5 days per week to meet your needs.
